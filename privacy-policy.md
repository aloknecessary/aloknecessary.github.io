# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-07-16

## Who I Am

This is a personal portfolio and blog operated by [Alok Ranjan Daftuar]({{ '/' | relative_url }}).

## What Data I Collect

### Analytics Cookies (Optional)

If you consent, I use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use this site. This helps me improve content and user experience.

**Google Analytics collects:**

- Pages you visit
- Time spent on pages
- Referral source (how you found the site)
- Device type and browser
- Approximate location (city/country level)
- Your IP address is anonymized

**Google Analytics does NOT collect:**

- Your name or email
- Precise location
- Personal identification

### Essential Technical Data

No cookies are set without your consent. Your browser automatically sends:

- IP address (for delivering web pages)
- Browser type
- Device type

This data is not stored by me.

## How I Use This Data

Analytics data helps me:

- Understand which blog posts are most helpful
- Improve site performance
- Create better content

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Decline analytics cookies (use the cookie banner)
- Change your mind anytime (click "Cookie Settings")
- Request deletion of your data (email me)
- Opt out via Global Privacy Control (GPC)

## Third-Party Services

### Google Analytics

- [Google Analytics Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

### Giscus (Comments)

Blog posts use [Giscus](https://giscus.app) for comments, powered by GitHub Discussions. If you choose to comment, you authenticate via your GitHub account. Giscus does not track you unless you interact with it.

- [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement)

### Google Fonts

This site loads fonts (Inter, JetBrains Mono) from Google Fonts. Your browser makes a request to Google's servers to fetch these fonts, which transmits your IP address.

- [Google Fonts Privacy FAQ](https://developers.google.com/fonts/faq/privacy)

## Data Retention

- Cookie consent choice: 1 year
- Google Analytics data: 14 months (Google's setting)

## Changes to This Policy

I may update this policy. The "Last Updated" date will reflect changes.
